Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 4, 2026

If you interacted with Guia PBEV Brasil through connected channels, including Instagram or Meta integrations, you may request deletion of your data associated with those interactions.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to fabio.pettian@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Data deletion request.
  3. Provide the information needed to locate the record, such as your Instagram @username and the approximate date of the interaction.

What will be deleted

  • Stored conversation and interaction records used for automation operations, when found.
  • Data linked to the user identifier used in the bot's operational context.

Processing time

The request will be reviewed and processed within a reasonable timeframe, subject to legal, technical, and security obligations. In some cases, minimal data may be retained when necessary for legal compliance or abuse prevention.

Contact

To follow up on the request, use: fabio.pettian@gmail.com.
